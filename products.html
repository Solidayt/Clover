Leaf Coasters

Each of these two ‘one of a kind’ 100% Absorbent Handmade Coasters is created using a blend of real leaves, seasonal wildflowers, and other unique local plants that are hand-picked from surrounding Virginia woodlands, artistically arranged, and hand rolled into our special blend of clay. The colors are artistically hand blended into each leaf and wildflower to bring out its unique natural beauty and the coaster is kiln fired for durability. There are no two coasters exactly alike.

Car Coasters

These car coasters are 100% Absorbent, handmade and Individually Hand-Colored. These coasters come as a unique set of 2, as shown above, and fit car sized cup holders that are 2 5/8" or larger at the base of the cup holder.

These car coasters are perfect for your car cup holders to absorb the drips that come off your cold drinks. They are also perfect for use in many golf cart cup holders.
